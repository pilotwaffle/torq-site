(torq-site-pi.vercel.app) by framing the product as the system that emerged from
Critical Path Methodology — not a separate chatbot layered on top.

Place the new section **immediately after "The Critical Path Methodology"** so the
methodology-to-system bridge reads naturally.

## Section 1 — Bridge section (new)

**Section title:** TORQ Console

**Body copy:**

TORQ Console is the system we built from years of real consulting work across
business operations, finance, and strategic execution.

It began with a simple constraint: human advisory bandwidth does not scale well
enough for modern business complexity. Critical Path Methodology helped us
identify what mattered most. TORQ Console was built to operationalize that
thinking into a governed system that can process more data, retain more context,
and produce more consistent decisions than traditional consulting alone.

It is not a chatbot layered onto a business.

It is a production intelligence system built to support advisory, document
review, strategy, execution, and continuous business improvement.

## Section 2 — How TORQ Works (new)

**Section title:** How TORQ Works

**Intro line:**

TORQ Console operates across five integrated layers:

**Layer list:**

- **Execution** — multi-agent workflows across tools and business data
- **Control** — real-time policy enforcement and risk-gated decisioning
- **Memory** — persistent system state, not chat history
- **Feedback** — structured telemetry tied to outcomes
- **Evolution** — continuous improvement with thresholds, rollback, and guardrails

**Closing line:**

This allows businesses to move beyond one-time recommendations and into systems
that can continuously operate, adapt, and improve.
